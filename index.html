🔍 Job Legitimacy Checker

Paste any LinkedIn job URL to analyze if it's legitimate or a potential scam

📖 How to Use This Tool

1
Find a job on LinkedIn

Go to LinkedIn and search for jobs. Click on any job posting you want to check.

2
Copy the job URL

From your browser's address bar, copy the full URL. It should look like:
https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/view/1234567890/

3
Paste and analyze

Paste the URL in the box below and click "Analyze Job". Wait a few seconds for results.

💡 Tip: Make sure the URL contains /jobs/view/ - this is a public job page. URLs with /jobs/collections/ are your personal recommendations and won't work.

Fetching job details and analyzing...

This may take 10-15 seconds

⚠️ This tool analyzes job descriptions for scam patterns using public data. Always do your own research before applying or sharing personal information.

🔒 All analysis happens in your browser. No data is stored on any server.
